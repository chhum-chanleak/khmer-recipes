Chicken Emerald Soup

Description

The dish is named because of the color from the snow peas which is similar to an emerald. We mostly see Chinese dishes using the snow peas either in soups or stir fries, and it is rare to use the peas in Cambodian cuisine; especially, with the paste which includes galangal, lemongrass, ka "r lime leaves, Asian basil leaves, and shrimp paste. !e taste from coconut cream combined with the paste from different ingredients, you will not be disappointed by spending time cooking it.
