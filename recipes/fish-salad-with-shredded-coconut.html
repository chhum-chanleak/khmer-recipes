Fish Salad With Shredded Coconut

Description

This starter dish is popular for special occasions in Cambodia: wedding receptions, dinner parties, or big family gatherings. This should be served fresh immediately after dressing. It is an unusual combination of ceviche fish and coconut meat. !e English translation of this dish’s name means "ceviche by the riverside” so consider serving the dish for a boating party.

Ingredients

  • 500 g/1.1 lb Snakehead fish fillets

  • 15 g/1 tbsp Kapi phao (see the recipe at the beginning of noodles section, note: can put the page number)

  • 50 g/1.8 oz Mixed basils (Thai, lemon, etc.)

  • 250 g/0.5 lb Shredded coconut meat

  • 50 g/1.8 oz Cilantro leaves

  • 50 g/1.8 oz Roasted peanuts

  • 175 ml/3⁄4 cup Lime juice

  • 10 g/2 tsp Salt

  • 15 ml/3 tsp Fish sauce

  • 25 g/4 tsp Palm sugar

Cooking instructions

  1. Grind the roasted peanuts finely. Heat a frying pan over medium heat, add he shredded coconut meat, and stir-fry for 5 mins until golden brown and smelling fragrant. Set it aside. Meanwhile, cut the fish paper-thin. Toss the fish with 150 ml of the lime juice and 5 g of salt in a mixing bowl (reserve the remaining 25 ml lime juice and 5 g salt for the dressing sauce).Mix the fish gently, and marinate until the flesh of the fish is firm and opaque, about 15 mins. Gently squeeze the fish to release about 80% of the lime juice.

  2. Transfer the squeezed fish into a medium mixing bowl, add the roasted coconut meat, kapi phao, fish sauce, palm sugar, and remaining salt and lime juice, then toss to combine evenly.

  3. To serve, place the salad on a large plate and right before serving garnish with ground peanuts, mixed herbs, and cilantro.
